About Us

We are Group 8 of A01-10-S2-1 from APEC Schools V. Luna. Our stream talks about the Essence of Mental Health Awareness to Junior High School Students. We focus on giving detailed information about the causes and effects of poor mental health, tips on how to prevent having poor mental health, and how we can help those who suffer from mental illness.
